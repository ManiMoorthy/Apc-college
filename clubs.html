About Anti Ragging Cell

Ragging in any form, either in words or in action is a crime as per supreme court orders. In order to curb the menace of ragging in higher educational Institutions, the college has an active Anti-Ragging Cell functioning from and it ensures that no such offence takes place within the premises. Dr. R. C. Vasuki, Principal, Dr. N. Meenakumari- Head & Associate Professor of Mathematics, Mrs. H. Kohila Subathra Christy – Head & Assistant Professor of Chemistry, Dr. R. Selvalatha- Assistant Professor of Tamil, Miss. M. Maheswari, Deputy Warden are the members of this cell.

Objectives:

  • To forbid ragging of any kind.
  • To raise awareness on the various causes that lead to ragging.
